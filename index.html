Processes in Operating Systems

A process is a program in execution, consisting of program code, data, and system resources.

Process Lifecycle

The process lifecycle includes states such as New, Ready, Running, Waiting, and Terminated.

Subprocesses

Subprocesses are child processes created by a parent, capable of concurrent execution.

Process Control Block (PCB)

The PCB holds information about a process, such as its ID, state, and memory details.
